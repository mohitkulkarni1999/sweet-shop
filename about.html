Our Story

Preserving tradition while innovating for the future

The Vida Paan Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a premium destination

Founded in 1995 by Master Paan Maker Rajiv Kapoor, Vida Paan Shop began as a small stall in Mumbai's bustling markets. What started as a passion for preserving traditional paan-making techniques quickly grew into a beloved establishment known for its quality and innovation.

Rajiv learned the art of paan-making from his grandfather, who was a renowned paan wallah in Varanasi. These century-old techniques, combined with Rajiv's innovative approach, form the foundation of our shop's philosophy.

Our Process

How we craft the perfect paan every time

1

Selecting the Finest Leaves

We source fresh betel leaves daily from trusted growers, ensuring only the most tender and aromatic leaves make it to your paan.

2

Preparing Traditional Masalas

Our signature spice blends are prepared in-house using family recipes and the highest quality ingredients.

3

Handcrafting Each Paan

Every paan is carefully assembled by our trained artisans who have mastered the delicate balance of flavors.

4

Quality Inspection

Before serving, each paan undergoes a final check to ensure it meets our high standards of taste and presentation.
