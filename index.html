Our Story

Tradition Meets Innovation

Founded in 1995, Vida Paan Shop has been serving authentic paan made with traditional recipes passed down through generations. We combine these time-honored techniques with modern flavors to create a unique paan experience.

Our master paan makers carefully select each ingredient, from the freshest betel leaves to premium saffron and gold leaf garnishes, ensuring every paan is a work of art.
